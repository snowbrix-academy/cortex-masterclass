The $75K-$150K Problem

Typical Scenario:

Your Snowflake invoice: $150,000 this month

CFO question: "Which teams are responsible for this spend?"

Your answer: "I don't know."

The Root Causes

  • Warehouse costs visible, but not attributed to teams/departments
  • Idle warehouses running 24/7 wasting 30-40% of spend
  • No real-time budget monitoring → surprise overruns at month-end
  • Finance demands chargeback, but platform teams lack tooling
  • Manual Excel exports from ACCOUNT_USAGE don't scale

The Solution: Enterprise FinOps Dashboard

Real-Time Cost Collection

  • Warehouse compute (per-minute grain)
  • Query-level costs with attribution
  • Storage (active + time-travel + failsafe)
  • Serverless (tasks, Snowpipe, MVs)
  • Cloud services (10% threshold rule)

Multi-Dimensional Chargeback

  • Direct attribution (warehouse → team)
  • Proportional (shared warehouses)
  • Tag-based (QUERY_TAG projects)
  • BI tool classification (Power BI, Tableau, dbt)

Automated Budget Controls

  • 3-tier alerts (80%, 90%, 100%)
  • Anomaly detection (z-score)
  • Month-end forecasting

Cost Optimization

  • Idle warehouse detection (>50%)
  • Warehouse sizing recommendations
  • Query optimization candidates
